Motivation: Demand vs. Price

you have historical sales: prices and quantity sold

you need to find find a price that maximizes total revenue

you build a model with price among other features and quantity sold as target

you find out that higher price leads to more sales

…

NO PROFIT, because low prices and low sales were there during crisis, and high prices and high sales happended during economic bloom

Causation

there is something that you can't find out given the historical data alone

you need to know that beforehand (domain knowledge) or run an experiment

kids learn that hard way (have you tried to wag dog's tail?)

Causal questions

  • Estimating the treatment effect on a population
  • Which treatment to choose for a patient
  • How much we would sell if we set price to X?
  • How much money your model deployment could bring?
  • Does X cause Y?
  • What if …?
  • And the most important question: will a dog be happy if we pet it?

this doesn't look like a typical supervised ML problem
